Not sure how to get your Google Review link? Here's how:

  1. On your phone or computer, search Google for your business name (or find it on Google Maps).
  2. Tap on your business listing so its full details open up.
  3. Tap the "Write a review" button - you don't need to actually write one.
  4. Copy the web address shown at the top of the page and paste it below in place of "https://".

If you manage your Google Business Profile, there's an even quicker way: sign in at business.google.com, find "Get more reviews", and copy the short link it gives you.

Not sure how to get your TripAdvisor link? Here's how:

  1. Go to tripadvisor.co.uk (or the TripAdvisor app) and search for your business name.
  2. Open your business's page from the search results.
  3. Tap the "Write a Review" button - you don't need to actually write one.
  4. Copy the web address shown at the top of the page and paste it below in place of "https://".

If you manage your TripAdvisor Business listing, you can also find this under "Management Centre" > "Get more reviews", which gives you a ready-made link to share.
